## 📌 Business Problem

Businesses lose revenue when they do not understand customer behavior.
This project solves that by identifying:
- High-value customers
- At-risk customers
- Revenue concentration patterns

---

## 🎯 Objective

Turn raw transaction data into actionable business insights using RFM segmentation.

---

## 🧠 Key Findings (IMPORTANT SECTION)

- A small group of customers contributes a large portion of revenue
- High-value customers are clearly identifiable using RFM scoring
- At-risk customers represent churn risk opportunities
- Revenue is heavily concentrated in top segments

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- Customer segmentation using RFM
- Interactive filtering by segment
- KPI metrics (Revenue, Customers, Averages)
- Customer distribution charts
- Top customer analysis
- Downloadable CSV export
- Executive PDF report generation

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Focus retention efforts on “Champions”
- Run re-engagement campaigns for “At Risk” customers
- Upsell “Loyal Customers” with bundled offers
- Prioritize marketing budget on high LTV segments
